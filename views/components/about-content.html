Ten years ago, I started Oliver’s Table with a sole goal. That goal was to prepare and serve food that tastes great. Having worked for 15 years as chef in two well established restaurants, I knew that preparing and serving delicious food is the rightful cornerstone of any truly successful restaurant. Great food begins with phenomenal ingredients.

A year before I started the restaurant, I traveled the world in search of food providers who grew crops without pesticide use, and who treated animals humanely the entire duration of their lives. While often unable to be stable suppliers due to the stringent ethical constraints they adopted, I knew these types of wholesome, ethical, and hardworking suppliers were the only ones I would trust to put food on our Table.

This lack of stability from the many suppliers required Oliver’s Table to have a diverse menu which was a daunting task. We adopted tablet menus to facilitate easy updates to customers about what was available. We also created a quick sanitization system to provide fresh, sterile and finger print free tablets to each set of guests.

While the technology at Oliver’s Table provides a modern aesthetic, you will still find a comfortable relaxed atmosphere where you can enjoy responsibly sourced food. Nothing is better than enjoying the highest quality food while being at ease among friends and family. We hope to see you soon at our Table.
